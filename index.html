How to use this dashboard

  1. Open a chapter by clicking its header. Read through each skill.
  2. Click the blue SOP links to open the written procedure (Word docs in the same folder).
  3. Click green Flowchart links to open visual flowcharts in your browser.
  4. Click purple Guide links for detailed system info.
  5. Rate your confidence using 1-2-3: 1 = need training, 2 = partially confident, 3 = confident.
  6. Tick the checkbox once you've read the SOP and understand the skill.
  7. Video links marked "Coming soon" will be replaced with Loom recordings as Ash records them.
  8. Write questions in the notes box at the bottom.
  9. Click Send Update to Ash to email your progress and notes directly.

Reviewer mode

  1. You're viewing a trainee's progress in read-only mode. Checkboxes and ratings can't be changed.
  2. Use the Reviewer Notes section at the bottom to leave your own feedback.
  3. Click Send Notes to Ash to email your feedback.
